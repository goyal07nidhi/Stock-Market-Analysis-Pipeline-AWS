About Us

Idea

Reddit is a social news platform that allows users to discuss and vote on content that other users have submitted. Recently, a group of online traders collaborated on Reddit & took down the hedge fund by bidding up the stock price of GameStop. This unprecedented move by the online traders took the market by storm with continuous ups and downs in the stock value. Therefore, we came up with an idea to analyze the discussions on Reddit for "/r/WallStreetBets" that will give the users an idea about what are some of the hot stocks that are being talked about and the sentiments of their discussion.

Also, we are leveraging yahoo finance stock data to provide current stock price, historical stock trend and forcasted price. You will find historical stock analysis dashboard in the Stock Trend Dashboard section.

Summary

To achieve the idea, we built the chatbot that will give an idea about the trending stocks that are being discussed on the Reddit platform along with the associated sentiment and other stock market trends. Chatbot gives the menu options for all the build features and also accepts users input to provide the assistance.
